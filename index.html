eStat is a web-based, dynamic graphical freeware aimed to provide better experience of statistics for students around the world.

eStat Middle School

eStat Middle School is specifically designed the statistics education of Middle School students. In this section, you can easily practice such statistical tools as Bar Graph, Pie Chart, Line Graph, Word Cloud, Stem & Leaf Plot, Histogram, and Correlation Coefficiencts.

eStat High School

eStat High School is primarily occupied with the further statistical tools offered for students studying in High School level. In this section, you can closely get to know numerous Theories of Probability, Law of Large Numbers, Dicrete, Binomial, Normal Distribution and much more.

eStat University

In eStat University, you can benefit from more advanced statistical tools along with the topics involving high and middle school level. In this section, eStat gives you the opportunity to master yourself in Estimations, Testing Hypothesis, Regression, Time Series and even, much more topics.
